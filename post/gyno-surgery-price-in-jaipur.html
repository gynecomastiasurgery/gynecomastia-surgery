FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

1. What is the cost of gyno surgery in Jaipur?

The cost of gynecomastia surgery varies depending on several factors, including the extent of the procedure, the surgical approach, and individual patient needs. During a personalized consultation at Panache Aesthetics, we will assess your condition and provide you with a detailed estimate tailored to your unique requirements.

2. Is gynecomastia surgery a permanent solution?

Yes, gynecomastia surgery offers lasting results. Once the excess breast tissue and fat are removed, the treated area should maintain its improved contour. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, including regular exercise and a balanced diet, can contribute to long-term success and overall well-being.

3. How long is the recovery period after the surgery?

Recovery times can vary based on the extent of the procedure and individual healing factors. Most patients can resume non-strenuous activities within a week after surgery. More rigorous activities, such as heavy lifting and intense exercise, may be gradually introduced after a few weeks to ensure proper healing and optimal results.

4. Are there any risks associated with gyno surgery?

As with any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery does carry potential risks, including infection, bleeding, scarring, and adverse reactions to anesthesia. However, these risks are minimized by adhering to our rigorous safety protocols, including thorough pre-operative evaluations and post-operative care instructions.

5. Can gynecomastia affect teenagers?

Yes, gynecomastia can indeed affect teenagers due to hormonal changes during puberty. While many cases resolve on their own as hormone levels stabilize, surgical intervention might be considered for individuals whose condition persists into adulthood. It’s important to consult with a skilled plastic surgeon to determine the appropriate course of action based on individual circumstances.
