Lipo Gynecomastia: Precision in Fat Removal

Lipo gynecomastia is a minimally invasive procedure that focuses on eliminating excess fat deposits in the chest area. This technique is particularly suitable for individuals whose Gynecomastia is primarily caused by surplus fat. Here’s how the process works:

  1. Consultation and Assessment: During the initial consultation, Dr. Vishal Purohit will evaluate your condition and determine whether lipo gynecomastia is the right approach for you. This assessment involves considering factors such as the amount of excess fat, skin elasticity, and overall health.
  2. Procedure: The procedure is performed under local anesthesia or intravenous sedation, ensuring your comfort throughout. Small incisions are strategically made to allow for the insertion of a thin tube called a cannula. The cannula is used to break down and suction out the excess fat deposits, sculpting a more contoured chest.
  3. Recovery: Following the procedure, patients can expect minimal discomfort and swelling. Recovery time is generally shorter compared to other surgical methods. You’ll be provided with specific aftercare instructions to promote healing and achieve optimal results.

Male Breast Gland Removal: Precision in Tissue Resection

For individuals with significant glandular tissue enlargement, male breast gland removal is the technique of choice. This procedure is designed to remove the glandular tissue responsible for the breast enlargement, resulting in a flatter and more masculine chest contour.

  1. Patient Evaluation: Dr. Vishal Purohit and his team will thoroughly assess your condition to determine whether male breast gland removal is the most suitable option. This evaluation considers the size and composition of the glandular tissue.
  2. Surgical Procedure: The surgery involves making discreet incisions to access the glandular tissue. The excess tissue is then carefully excised, creating a smoother chest contour. The incisions are closed meticulously, ensuring minimal scarring.
  3. Recovery and Results: Recovery from male breast gland removal may take slightly longer compared to lipo gynecomastia due to the nature of tissue resection. However, the results are well worth the investment, offering a more sculpted and confident chest appearance.

What causes Gynecomastia?

Gynecomastia can result from various factors, including hormonal imbalances during puberty, certain medications, obesity, or genetics. It’s essential to undergo a thorough evaluation by a qualified plastic surgeon to determine the underlying cause of your condition.

Is the procedure painful?

Gynecomastia Surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring that you remain comfortable throughout the procedure. Post-operative discomfort is typically manageable with prescribed pain medication, and any discomfort will gradually diminish as you recover.

What is the recovery process like?

Recovery experiences vary among individuals, but most patients experience mild soreness, swelling, and bruising. These effects are temporary and should subside over time. Following post-operative care instructions, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and attending follow-up appointments contribute to a smoother recovery.

Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of Gynecomastia Surgery are typically long-lasting. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to ensure the best outcomes. Fluctuations in weight and hormonal changes can potentially impact the results over time.
