FAQs: Answering Your Questions About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

1. Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

Absolutely. Gynecomastia surgery is generally safe when performed by a skilled and experienced plastic surgeon. At my clinic in Jaipur, your safety is our top priority. We conduct thorough pre-operative assessments and adhere to best practices to ensure a safe and successful procedure.

2. What is the recovery period after surgery?

Recovery times can vary, but most patients can expect to resume normal activities within a few weeks after gynecomastia surgery. During your post-operative appointments, I’ll provide you with personalized recovery guidelines to support your healing process and optimize your results.

3. Will there be visible scars?

While some scarring is inevitable, I use advanced surgical techniques to minimize the visibility of scars. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas whenever possible. Over time, scars tend to fade and become less noticeable, allowing you to enjoy the results of your surgery without the concern of prominent scarring.

4. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically permanent. The removal of excess breast tissue and fat during the procedure helps create a more contoured and masculine chest appearance. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle and avoid significant fluctuations in weight to ensure the longevity of your results.

5. Can exercise alone eliminate gynecomastia?

In some cases, exercise and diet may help improve the appearance of gynecomastia, especially if the condition is caused by excess fat. However, true gynecomastia often involves glandular tissue that cannot be addressed through exercise alone. Surgical intervention, such as gynecomastia surgery, provides more comprehensive and targeted results.
