FAQs - Commonly Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As a seasoned plastic surgeon specializing in gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, I understand that you may have questions about the procedure, recovery, and outcomes. Here are answers to some commonly asked questions to help you make informed decisions:

1. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring that you are comfortable during the procedure. Post-surgery, any discomfort can be managed effectively with prescribed pain medications. Rest assured, my team and I prioritize your comfort throughout the surgical journey.

2. How long is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

Recovery times can vary based on the extent of the procedure and your body’s healing response. In most cases, patients can expect to return to work within a week. However, it’s important to avoid strenuous activities for a few weeks to ensure proper healing and optimal results.

3. Are there any risks associated with gynecomastia surgery?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries potential risks. During consultations, I thoroughly discuss these risks with patients to ensure they have a clear understanding of what to expect. With proper pre-operative assessments, skilled techniques, and attentive post-operative care, risks can be minimized.

4. Will there be visible scars after surgery?

I understand concerns about scarring, and I take steps to minimize their visibility. Incisions are typically made in inconspicuous areas, allowing scars to be less noticeable over time. Additionally, I provide guidance on scar care to help facilitate the healing process.

5. Can gynecomastia recur after surgery?

When gynecomastia surgery is performed by an experienced surgeon and patients adhere to post-operative instructions, the chances of recurrence are minimal. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle and manage any underlying hormonal imbalances to ensure the best long-term outcomes.
