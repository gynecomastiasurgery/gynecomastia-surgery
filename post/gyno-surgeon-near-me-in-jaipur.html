Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

Gynecomastia surgery is generally safe when performed by a skilled and experienced surgeon like myself. During your consultation, we’ll discuss your medical history and any potential risk factors to ensure that you’re a suitable candidate for the procedure.

How long is the recovery period after surgery?

Recovery time can vary depending on the extent of the surgery and your body’s healing process. Most patients can resume light activities within a week after surgery. However, it’s important to avoid strenuous exercise and heavy lifting for a few weeks to allow your body to heal properly. I will provide you with detailed post-operative care instructions to facilitate a smooth recovery.

Are the results of surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically permanent, provided you maintain a healthy lifestyle and stable weight. The excess breast tissue and fat that are removed during surgery do not typically grow back. However, it’s important to note that significant fluctuations in weight can impact the appearance of your chest over time.

Will there be noticeable scars after surgery?

I understand the importance of minimizing visible scarring. I use advanced surgical techniques to strategically place incisions in inconspicuous areas. Over time, these incisions tend to fade and become less noticeable. Additionally, I provide guidance on scar care to promote optimal healing and reduce the visibility of scars.
