FAQs: Commonly Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Here are answers to five frequently asked questions about gynecomastia surgery:

1. What causes gynecomastia?

Gynecomastia can be triggered by a variety of factors, including hormonal imbalances, genetics, certain medications, and medical conditions. Hormonal changes during puberty or aging can lead to an imbalance between testosterone and estrogen, resulting in the enlargement of breast tissue in males. It’s essential to undergo a thorough evaluation with a specialist to determine the underlying cause of your gynecomastia.

2. Is gynecomastia surgery a permanent solution?

Yes, gynecomastia surgery offers a long-lasting solution. During the procedure, excess breast tissue is removed, resulting in a flatter and more contoured chest. Barring any significant hormonal fluctuations or weight changes, the results of gyno removal surgery are generally permanent. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to ensure the best outcome over time.

3. How long is the recovery period after surgery?

The recovery period varies from individual to individual and depends on the extent of the surgery. Generally, you can expect to return to work and light activities within a week or two. Strenuous activities and exercise should be avoided for several weeks to ensure proper healing. I provide comprehensive post-operative instructions to guide you through a smooth recovery process.

4. Will there be visible scars after the procedure?

While gynecomastia surgery does involve incisions, I use advanced techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas to ensure that any resulting scars are as discreet as possible. Over time, scars tend to fade and become less noticeable, allowing you to enjoy the results without significant visible marks.

5. Are the results of gyno removal surgery natural-looking?

Absolutely. One of my primary goals as a specialist is to achieve results that appear natural and harmonious with your body. Through careful planning and meticulous surgical techniques, I aim to create a chest contour that aligns with your masculine features. The goal is not only to remove excess breast tissue but also to create a balanced and aesthetically pleasing appearance.
