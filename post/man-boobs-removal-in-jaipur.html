Gynecomastia Surgery FAQs

As a leading plastic surgeon specializing in gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, I understand that you may have questions about the procedure, recovery, and expected outcomes. Here are five commonly asked questions about gynecomastia surgery, along with informative answers to guide you in making informed decisions.

1. How long does the recovery process usually take?

The recovery period after gynecomastia surgery varies depending on the extent of the procedure and individual factors. Generally, you can expect to resume light activities within a week, but it’s essential to avoid strenuous exercise for a few weeks to ensure proper healing. I provide personalized post-operative instructions to help you navigate the recovery process smoothly.

2. Are the results of gyno surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically permanent. Once the excess glandular tissue and fat are removed, your chest’s improved contour should remain stable over time. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle and stable weight can contribute to long-lasting results.

3. What are the potential risks and complications associated with the procedure?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries potential risks and complications. These can include infection, bleeding, asymmetry, scarring, or changes in nipple sensation. However, with proper surgical techniques and post-operative care, these risks are minimized. During your consultation, I’ll thoroughly discuss potential risks and how we mitigate them.

4. Will there be noticeable scarring after the surgery?

Efforts are made to minimize scarring as much as possible. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas, and I use techniques to ensure scars fade over time. While some degree of scarring is unavoidable, they usually become less noticeable as you heal and the tissue settles.

5. Is gynecomastia surgery suitable for all body types?

Gynecomastia surgery can be adapted to various body types, but a personalized approach is crucial. During your consultation, I’ll evaluate your individual needs and recommend the most suitable surgical technique based on factors such as your gynecomastia type, body composition, and aesthetic goals.
