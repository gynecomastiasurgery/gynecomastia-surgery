Dr. Vishal Purohit recognizes the significance of addressing this concern and offers expert insights into the world of pseudogynecomastia surgery in Jaipur.

Tailored Solutions for Pseudogynecomastia

Understanding that each individual’s body is unique, Dr. Purohit approaches pseudogynecomastia surgery with a personalized perspective. During your consultation, he evaluates your specific case and designs a treatment plan that caters to your needs. The procedure typically involves liposuction, a minimally invasive technique that targets and removes excess fat from the chest area. Through small incisions, a thin tube called a cannula is inserted to suction out the fat, resulting in a more sculpted chest contour.

Patients are encouraged to wear compression garments as advised to minimize swelling and support the healing process. The results gradually become noticeable as the swelling subsides, revealing a more defined chest appearance. Engaging in light physical activities can often be resumed within a few weeks, depending on individual recovery progress.
