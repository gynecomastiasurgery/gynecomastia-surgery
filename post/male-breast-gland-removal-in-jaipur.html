Types of Gynecomastia

There are various types of gynecomastia, each with its own characteristics and causes. Understanding the type of gynecomastia you’re dealing with is crucial in determining the most appropriate treatment approach. Here are a few common types:

  1. True Gynecomastia: This type is characterized by the growth of glandular breast tissue beneath the areola, leading to a firm, rubbery mass.
  2. Pseudogynecomastia: Unlike true gynecomastia, this type is caused by an accumulation of fat rather than glandular tissue. It results in a softer and more diffused chest appearance.
