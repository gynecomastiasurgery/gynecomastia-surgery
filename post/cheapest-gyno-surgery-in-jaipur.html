Types of Gynecomastia in Jaipur

Gynecomastia isn’t a one-size-fits-all condition. It comes in various forms, each with its unique underlying causes and characteristics. In Jaipur, where diversity is celebrated, it’s essential to understand the range of gynecomastia types to tailor treatment approaches effectively.

1. Hormonal Imbalance-Induced Gynecomastia

Hormonal fluctuations are a common trigger for gynecomastia, especially during puberty or as a result of medical conditions. This type often involves temporary breast tissue enlargement due to an imbalance in estrogen and testosterone levels. It usually subsides over time as hormones stabilize.

2. Pseudo-Gynecomastia

Pseudo-gynecomastia, often referred to as “false gynecomastia,” occurs due to an excess accumulation of fat in the chest area. Unlike glandular tissue enlargement, this type is primarily caused by weight gain and excess body fat. Exercise and dietary modifications can help address this variation.

3. Glandular Gynecomastia

Glandular gynecomastia involves the enlargement of breast tissue caused by an overgrowth of glandular tissue. This type can result from various factors such as genetics, medication side effects, or underlying medical conditions. Surgical intervention may be required to remove the excess glandular tissue and restore a masculine chest contour.
