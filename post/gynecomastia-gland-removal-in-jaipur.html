FAQs About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

If you’re considering gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, you likely have questions about the procedure, recovery, and expected outcomes. Here are answers to five commonly asked questions to help you make an informed decision:

1. What causes gynecomastia?

Gynecomastia can occur due to hormonal imbalances, often during puberty or as a result of certain medical conditions. Medications, such as those used for prostate conditions or anti-depressants, can also contribute to gynecomastia. By addressing the underlying cause and tailoring the treatment approach, gynecomastia surgery can effectively reduce or eliminate the enlarged breast tissue.

2. Is surgery the only solution for gynecomastia treatment?

While non-surgical approaches like lifestyle changes or hormonal therapy might work for some cases, gynecomastia surgery is often the most effective way to achieve permanent results. Surgical techniques, such as gland removal and liposuction, can precisely target excess tissue and reshape the chest for a more masculine contour.

3. What is the recovery process like after gland removal surgery?

The recovery period following gland removal surgery varies from person to person. Most patients can resume light activities within a week, and more strenuous activities can typically be resumed within a few weeks. Wearing a compression garment is often recommended to aid in healing and reduce swelling.

4. Will there be visible scars after surgery?

Gynecomastia surgery incisions are made strategically to minimize scarring. Over time, scars tend to fade significantly and become less noticeable. Following post-operative care instructions and avoiding sun exposure can further promote favorable scar healing.

5. Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent, especially when combined with a healthy lifestyle. It’s important to maintain a stable weight and adopt a fitness routine to ensure the best long-term outcomes.
