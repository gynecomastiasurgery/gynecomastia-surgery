FAQ’s About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As you consider gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, it’s natural to have questions about the procedure, recovery, and results. Here, we address five frequently asked questions to provide you with valuable insights into the world of gynecomastia removal.

1. What is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

The recovery period after gynecomastia surgery can vary based on the extent of the procedure and individual healing rates. Typically, patients can expect to return to their regular activities within a week or two after the surgery. However, it’s important to avoid strenuous exercises and heavy lifting for a few weeks to allow your body to heal properly.

2. Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. Once the excess breast tissue and fat are removed, they do not grow back. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle and stable weight is essential to ensure the long-term success of the procedure.

3. Will there be noticeable scarring after the procedure?

Gynecomastia surgery is designed to minimize visible scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas, such as around the areola. Over time, these scars tend to fade and become less noticeable.

4. Can gynecomastia recur even after surgical treatment?

While gynecomastia surgery provides lasting results, there is a small possibility of recurrence, especially if hormonal imbalances or other underlying factors persist. Consulting with an experienced plastic surgeon like Dr. Vishal Purohit can help you understand the likelihood of recurrence in your specific case.

5. Are there any risks or complications associated with the surgery?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries certain risks and complications. However, these risks are minimal and rare when performed by a qualified and experienced plastic surgeon.
