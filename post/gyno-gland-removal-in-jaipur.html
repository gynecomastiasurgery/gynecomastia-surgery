FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Navigating the world of gyno gland removal surgery can raise a multitude of questions. In this section, I address some of the most commonly asked questions about gynecomastia surgery, providing you with the insights you need to make informed decisions.

Gyno gland removal surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring your comfort throughout the procedure. As your body heals, you’ll notice the discomfort gradually subsiding, allowing you to focus on your recovery and transformation.

2. What is the cost of gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur?

The cost of gyno gland removal surgery varies based on several factors, including the extent of the procedure, the need for additional treatments such as liposuction, and whether you opt for combined procedures. During your personalized consultation, we provide a detailed breakdown of the costs involved, ensuring transparency and allowing you to make an informed decision.

3. How long is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

Most individuals can return to non-strenuous activities within a week after gyno gland removal surgery. While complete recovery varies from person to person, it typically takes a few weeks to resume all regular activities. Engaging in a healthy lifestyle, following post-operative instructions, and attending follow-up appointments contribute to a smoother recovery.

4. Are the results of gyno gland removal permanent?

Yes, in most cases, the results of gyno gland removal are permanent. The procedure involves removing excess glandular tissue, resulting in a flatter and more contoured chest. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle and managing factors such as weight fluctuations contribute to sustaining the results over the long term.

5. Are there any risks associated with gynecomastia surgery?

Like any surgical procedure, gyno gland removal surgery carries minimal risks. Complications are rare, but potential risks include infection, bleeding, scarring, and adverse reactions to anesthesia.
