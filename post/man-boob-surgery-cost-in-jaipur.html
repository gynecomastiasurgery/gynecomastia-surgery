Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) About Gynecomastia Surgery

As you contemplate gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, you likely have questions and concerns. Let’s address some of the most common queries to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of what to expect:

1. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Pain levels can vary from person to person, but rest assured that steps are taken to minimize discomfort during and after the procedure. Local anesthesia or sedation is administered to ensure you’re comfortable during surgery. Post-surgery, any discomfort can be managed with prescribed pain medication and proper care.

2. Will there be visible scars after surgery?

Efforts are made to minimize scarring through strategic incision placement and advanced techniques. Scarless surgery aims to leave little to no visible scarring. In cases where incisions are necessary, they are typically made in discreet locations, allowing scars to fade over time.

3. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery times can vary depending on the type of surgery and individual factors. Generally, patients can expect to resume normal activities within a week or two, with full recovery taking a few weeks to a couple of months. Adhering to post-operative instructions and attending follow-up appointments are crucial for a smooth recovery.

4. Can gynecomastia recur after surgery?

In most cases, gynecomastia surgery provides long-lasting results. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, managing weight fluctuations, and addressing any underlying hormonal imbalances can help minimize the risk of recurrence.

5. What is the cost of gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur?

The cost of gynecomastia surgery varies based on factors such as the type of surgery, surgeon’s experience, facility fees, and post-operative care. During your consultation, I will provide you with a comprehensive cost breakdown tailored to your specific needs.
