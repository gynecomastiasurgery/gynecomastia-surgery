Q1: What is the recovery time post-surgery?

A: Recovery times can vary, but most patients typically resume light activities within a week after surgery. More strenuous activities may be gradually reintroduced within a few weeks. It’s important to follow post-operative instructions to ensure a smooth recovery process.

Q2: Will there be visible scarring?

A: While surgery involves incisions, I employ advanced techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed and often heal discreetly over time, resulting in minimal visible scarring and a more natural appearance.

Q3: Can gynecomastia recur after surgery?

A: While gynecomastia recurrence is rare, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle and avoid significant weight fluctuations. Adhering to a balanced diet and exercise routine can help prevent the return of excess breast tissue.

Q4: Are the results permanent?

A: Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically long-lasting, especially when combined with a stable weight post-surgery. The removal of excess tissue and contouring of the chest contribute to lasting improvements.

Q5: Is the procedure painful?

A: Discomfort is manageable with prescribed pain medications during the initial recovery period. As healing progresses, any discomfort gradually diminishes. My team and I are committed to ensuring your comfort throughout the surgical process.
