Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring that you’re comfortable throughout the procedure. While some discomfort and mild pain can be expected post-operatively, these sensations are manageable with prescribed pain medications. Our team is committed to minimizing any potential discomfort and ensuring a smooth recovery process.

How long is the recovery period after surgery?

Recovery times can vary based on individual factors and the extent of the procedure. In most cases, patients can resume light activities within a week after surgery. However, it’s recommended to avoid strenuous activities for about 4 to 6 weeks to ensure proper healing. During your post-operative consultations, we will provide guidance on when it’s safe to resume different levels of activity.

Will there be visible scars after the surgery?

Incisions made during gynecomastia surgery are strategically placed to minimize visible scarring. Over time, these scars tend to fade significantly, becoming less noticeable. Our skilled surgical techniques and attention to detail aim to achieve results that not only improve your chest’s appearance but also ensure a natural look and minimal scarring.

Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent, provided a healthy lifestyle is maintained. Significant weight fluctuations or hormonal imbalances can potentially affect the long-term results. By maintaining a stable weight and following post-operative care instructions, you can enjoy lasting outcomes that align with your desired appearance.

When can I expect to see the final results?

While initial results become visible after swelling subsides, usually around 4 to 6 weeks post-surgery, the final results become more apparent as your body continues to heal and adjust. Over the course of a few months, you will notice the full impact of the surgery, with a more defined and masculine chest contour.
