The phenomenon of puffy nipples after gyno surgery is often related to residual glandular tissue or excess skin in the treated area. This can result from various factors, including the original extent of gynecomastia, the surgical technique used, and the body’s natural healing process. Dr. Purohit emphasizes that while puffy nipples can be a cause of concern, they are not uncommon and can be effectively addressed through appropriate measures.
