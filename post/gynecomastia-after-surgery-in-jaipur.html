FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As you consider gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, it’s natural to have questions about the procedure, recovery, and results. Here are some frequently asked questions to provide you with a better understanding of what to expect:

Q: What is the recovery time after gynecomastia surgery?

A: Recovery times can vary depending on the extent of the surgery and individual healing rates. Most patients can resume light activities within a week after the procedure. However, it’s important to avoid strenuous activities and heavy lifting for a few weeks to ensure proper healing.

Q: Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

A: While some discomfort is normal after surgery, pain is generally manageable with prescribed medications. Our team will provide you with the necessary pain relief and guidance to ensure your comfort throughout the recovery process.

Q: Can gynecomastia recur after surgery?

A: The likelihood of gynecomastia recurrence is low, especially when the surgery is performed by skilled surgeons. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle and managing factors that contributed to the condition can further reduce the risk of recurrence.

Q: Are the results of surgery permanent?

A: Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. Once excess breast tissue is removed, the contours of your chest are improved. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to support long-lasting outcomes.

Q: Are there any potential complications of gynecomastia surgery?

A: Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries potential risks. These can include infection, scarring, asymmetry, and changes in nipple sensation. Our skilled surgical team takes extensive precautions to minimize these risks and ensure your safety and satisfaction.
