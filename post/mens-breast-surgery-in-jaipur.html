Understanding Gynecomastia

Gynecomastia, often referred to as enlarged male breasts, is a condition that can impact men of all ages. It occurs when there is an increase in the size of breast tissue in males, leading to a chest appearance that may resemble that of a woman’s breasts. This condition can be caused by a variety of factors, including hormonal imbalances, genetics, obesity, certain medications, and underlying medical conditions. The emotional and psychological impact of gynecomastia can be significant, affecting self-confidence and overall quality of life.

Types of Gynecomastia

Gynecomastia presents in different forms, each requiring a tailored approach to treatment. However, more severe cases may involve significant enlargement of breast tissue, excess fat deposition, or a combination of both. In such instances, surgical intervention may be the recommended course of action.

Yes, gynecomastia surgery is usually performed under general anesthesia to ensure your comfort and safety throughout the procedure. Dr. Purohit and his experienced team will oversee the anesthesia process, ensuring a smooth and stress-free surgical experience.
