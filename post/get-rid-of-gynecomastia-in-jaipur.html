Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring your comfort throughout the procedure. I work closely with each patient to ensure that their pain is minimized, and their recovery is as comfortable as possible.

2. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery times can vary based on individual factors and the extent of the procedure. Most patients can expect to return to their regular activities within a few weeks after surgery. However, it’s important to note that more strenuous activities and exercise might need to be limited for a slightly longer period to allow for proper healing and optimal results.

3. Will the results look natural?

Absolutely. My goal is to provide you with results that look natural and harmonious. The surgical techniques I use are specifically designed to create a balanced chest contour that appears natural. 5. Are there any risks involved?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries some inherent risks.
